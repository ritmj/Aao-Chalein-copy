Sa-Haive

“Sa-Haive” is a web based application which pairs up individuals from the same Institution based upon their common destination and mutually agreeable time interval. The platform has been built keeping in mind the remote institutions with a significant number of commuters. The application shall also provide the travelers with contact details of verified drivers for that area. It can also help the students plan any trip to/from the institution in advance by looking up at the database to identify the co-travellers. The application thus comes with a commitment to cut down an individual's traveling expenditures and bring in a sense of safety to the travelers.

The project was born out of inconveniences we faced at our own Campus, IIT Kanpur. With more than 9K+ individuals onboard at this remote location and multiple commuters round the clock, the institution is a good fit for our analysis. The portal is currently going through the testing phase at our Campus and we shall be working upon further improvements and advances with the course of time.
Route Anomaly Alert

This application shall alert the user in case the driver is taking an unusual route to the destination. In remote areas with sparse populations, this is one of the major safety concerns for most of us. In case an unfavorable situation builds up, the user can alert the relevant authorities through this portal . The idea is currently in its initial phase and shall be added as an extension to the application "Sa-Haive" upon its Completion.
Kon-Act

At Institutions as large as our own, there is a plethora of individuals working upon pretty much all the domains known to humanity. However, what we found somewhat missing was a platform to connect the willing peers together who could then together work in unison. At IITK, we are still fortunate enough to have clubs, societies and interest groups which is however not the case at a majority of institutions. Moreover, such connections cross-cutting through the physical boundaries of their respective institutions is extremely rare. “Kon-act” is a project we have been working upon keeping this issue in mind, aiming to unite all the interest groups who could be a help to each other, together.
